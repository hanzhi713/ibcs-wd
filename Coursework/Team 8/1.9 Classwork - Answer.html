Question 1: Explain why is it important to have GPUs and large labeled data sets in order to train CNNs [2]

Because training of CNN involves large amount of matrix calculations, so GPUs’ high calculating speed is important. Large labeled data sets are used to adjust the parameters in the fully connected layers in the network. If only small amount of data is provided, the CNN may not be able to be adjusted to the optimal state.
