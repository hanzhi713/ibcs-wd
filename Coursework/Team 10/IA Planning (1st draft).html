Defining the problem

My mother (my client), who has worked as an accountant for more than 20 years, has a habit of keeping accounts of the incomes and outgoings in our home. However she realized that after years of the traditional approach: designing the from, handwriting or typing to put all the numbers into a form, and calculations the result, it is way too complicated in a digital age. So she hopes that there can be a software to keep track of a family’s accounting situation and help people with organizing their financial status, especially when they want a surplus by the end of each month.

Success criteria

  • Allow the user to input incomes and outgoings easily for any number of times.
  • Do calculations quickly about the accounting situation for the family in accordance to the users’ needs.
  • Draw statistical charts and graphs whenever users need and by the end of each month.
  • Give suggestions to users according to the proportion of money spending with statistics stored.

Rationale for the proposed product

I decided to use Java for the following reasons:
  • I am familiar with java
  • It runs on a considerable number of different platforms
  • It has a good development environment
  • It is object oriented so methods can be written and tested separately to allow each of them to work effectively
  • It allows the existence of encapsulation which can protect the privacy of the users, in this case especially because no one would want his/her family’s financial status to be shown to others or to be changed accidentally
