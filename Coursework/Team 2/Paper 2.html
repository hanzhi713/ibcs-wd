A investigation into paper 2


Paper 2 is all about OOP, which is an option. Almost every problem in this paper has big chunks of texts, mostly code, or some UML diagrams. The questions are mostly about analyzing the code. Some questions are about definitions, and I believe this kind of questions is the easiest amongst all, for this kind of questions just asks you to memorize the definitions.

There are also questions asking you to construct codes. This is not very hard. We just need to first construct an algorithm inside our brain or do some scratch on paper, or maybe draw a UML diagrams.

A kind of question that is harder is "outline". "Outline" asks us to first define some specific terms, then elaborates the impact of those terms, and then makes up some examples to further aid the explanation.

UML diagrams are also very hardcore. We have to master the diagram to illustrate the plan of our code.
