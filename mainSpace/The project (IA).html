Internal Assessment (from http://ib.compscihub.net/internal-assessment/example-ias )


  • The Computer Science Internal Assessment (IA) is officially referred to as ‘The Solution‘.
  • It involves using Computing technology to solve a problem for a real-life client.
  • The IA accounts for 30% of the total Standard Level (SL) grade and 20% of the Higher Level (HL) grade.
  • The IA has 5 parts – click here to learn about the different parts (A-E)
  • Please note that from May 2016 all IAs have to be uploaded digitally to the IBO for assessment.
  • Submissions must be ZIPPED for each student and be no more than 750MB.
