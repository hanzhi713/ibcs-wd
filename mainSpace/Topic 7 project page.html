Topic 7 project page #topic7

In topic 7, you are expected to independently complete an arduino project doing controls! This is going to be a major assessment.
Get your hands dirty! You will love your project!

Assessment Requirement:
The project must be a control system!!!
You are required to make a log video of how you build your project with illustrations.
the video includes:
  • project aim
  • project materials needed
  • project design
  • project work process
  • project challenging problems
  • project demonstration
  • What you've learned about control
The length of the video: around 5 minutes
You will be given:
  • project start time: Oct 10
  • project work time: Oct 17, 18, 24, 25 four times of lessons for your project, each time will be about 1 hour ( 20 minutes for another presentation project)
  • Deadline: Oct 25. submit your final project prototype and video
  • Exhibition time: Oct. 31 -- we will have a demo lesson based on your control projects!
